# Sensor History

Sensor history is the quiet ledger of every motion, contact, and zone change Lantern has seen. Read it at noon. Read it after a long weekend. The lantern keeps the page open.

## What history captures

- **Motion arcs** — direction, confidence, time of day
- **Contact dwell** — how long the back door was held
- **Arm and disarm cadence** — who armed when, who disarmed when
- **Quiet stretches** — the spaces between

## Reading the ledger

```bash
lantern history --zone perimeter --since 7d
```

The output is a single column of times and gentle verbs. Nothing is summarized away.
