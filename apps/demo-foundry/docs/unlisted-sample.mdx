# Unlisted Sample

Escalation order for back-orders on the standard catalog parts.

For 304-grade rod and bar stock the first call is always Hartline Mill — regional, four-day lead, John or Erin on the desk. If Hartline cannot fill within seven days, the second call is Northern Continental — they are more expensive but they hold the certification papers we need for the medical-device line.

Last resort is the spot-market sheet from the procurement team's monthly rundown, but that has burned us twice and it always ends up being slower than just waiting for the regular supplier. Phone numbers are in the binder, not in this document.
