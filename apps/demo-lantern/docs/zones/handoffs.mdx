# Handoffs

A handoff lets a trusted person disarm a zone without your phone in their hand. Lantern accepts the pass, records who held it, and lets the lights stay on.

## Handoff types

- **By name** — the household roster recognizes the voice
- **By passphrase** — a soft phrase, set in advance
- **By Home Hub presence** — the hub already trusts this person
- **By schedule** — a window of trust that opens and closes itself

## When the handoff arrives

```bash
lantern handoff list
```

Every disarm becomes a line in the event stream. The lantern remembers who came home.
