# Welcome to Marshal

Marshal is the Department of Repository Affairs — automated stewardship for projects that prefer paperwork to drama.

Filed in triplicate. Sealed in good faith. Stamped before noon.

## What you can do

- File policy workflows for any repository under your stewardship
- Archive inactive threads on a schedule, with a paper trail
- Gate support and triage by access tier, without lifting a pen
- Record every action in the public ledger — nothing closes silently

There is nothing here a clerk in 1962 would not have recognized.
