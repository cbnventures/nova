# Unlisted Sample

This is not part of the published documentation, but it is what we hand to readers who ask "where do I start" after they have finished the setup guide.

First on the list is always Tanizaki's *In Praise of Shadows*, which has nothing to do with home automation but reframes how to think about lighting as atmosphere rather than illumination.

Second is Bachelard's *Poetics of Space* — every chapter has something to say about thresholds, which is exactly what a smart lock is. Third is whichever current edition of *Make* magazine has the home-electronics issue.

The list is opinionated. That is the point.
