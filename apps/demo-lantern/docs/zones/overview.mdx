# Zones

A zone is a named room, hallway, exterior, or appliance group that Lantern watches together. Group your devices by where they live, not by what they are.

## Default zones

- **Perimeter** — exterior doors, windows, gates
- **Interior** — hallways, stairwells, common rooms
- **Watch** — sleeping areas, after dark
- **Quiet** — bedrooms, bathrooms — armed only on travel mode

## Arming a zone

Arm any zone independently. Disarm gently, by name. Override with a phrase. Lantern never raises its voice.
