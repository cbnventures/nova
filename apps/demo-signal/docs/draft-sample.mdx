# Draft Sample

The conversion-attribution job has been throwing seven-day moving averages that include the launch-week spike, which means everything downstream is comparing against a recent peak that should really be excluded from the baseline.

The choice is either to backfill a synthetic baseline that linearly bridges the pre-launch and post-launch windows, or to trim the launch week from the rolling window entirely. Notebook sketches say trimming is cleaner and easier to explain to product.

We will write up a one-pager and circulate before touching the cron. Not committing this until Eng-Data signs off.
