# Welcome to Lantern

Lantern is a whole-house monitoring toolkit for HomeKit hubs that prefer warm light to flashing red.

The whole house, gently held. From the front porch to the back hallway, every zone is watched without ever feeling watched.

## What you can do

- Discover and arm devices in any zone
- Stream motion events as they happen
- Browse sensor history without leaving the room
- Hand off arm/disarm to someone you trust at 2am

There is nothing to configure that you would not want a porch light to know.
