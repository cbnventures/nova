# Scheduled Stewardship

Marshal runs scheduled work on a cron. Cleanups, audits, rollovers, label sweeps — anything that should happen at the top of the hour without anyone being woken up.

## What can be scheduled

- **Cleanups** — close and seal threads that meet a policy condition
- **Audits** — verify that every open ticket has been classified
- **Rollovers** — roll the docket forward into the next quarter
- **Label sweeps** — apply or remove labels in bulk

## Filing a scheduled job

```yaml title="schedules/quarterly-rollover.yml"
schedule: "0 0 1 1,4,7,10 *"
action: rollover
docket: "FY-{quarter}"
notice: "Rolled forward at the top of the quarter."
```

## Watching the cron

```bash
marshal cron list
```

Marshal lists every scheduled job, its next run, and the policy that filed it. Nothing runs unsealed.
